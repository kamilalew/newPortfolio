Why Founders and Coders

At the beginning of 2018, roughly at the same time that I decided to follow my dreams, I have found a couple of web development bootcamps in Poland that would help me to move into career I always wanted to pursue. At first it was meant to be a front-end followed by a back-end with PHP. Both based on working in class with the tutor. Only then I found Founders and Coders and decided to swap the back-end in Krakow for the course offered by Founders & Coders. What got me interested is the collaborative environment you're promising and the fact that I can take part in training others. I think this is a great way to learn self-motivation and self-learning rather than relying on the tutor. This is a great asset in the always changing development field. '
